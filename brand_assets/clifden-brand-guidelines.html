✓
Use approved logo files only

Always download and use the original logo files provided. Do not recreate or redraw the logo.

✓
Maintain minimum clear space

Surround the logo with at least the equivalent height of the "C" in Clifden as clear space on all sides.

✗
Do not stretch or distort

Always scale the logo proportionally. Never stretch, compress, skew, or otherwise distort the logo mark.

✗
Do not change logo colours

Only use the four approved logo colour variations shown above. Do not apply drop shadows, outlines, or effects.

✗
Do not place on busy backgrounds

Ensure the logo always sits on a clean, single-colour background with sufficient contrast.

Colour Usage Rules

  • Gold — Use sparingly for key accents, CTAs, underlines and dividers. Never overuse.
  • Dark Brown — Reserved for headings, dark backgrounds, and premium contexts.
  • Cream — Primary page and section background. Warmer and more refined than white.
  • Charcoal — Default body text. Do not use pure black (#000) for text.

04 — Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

We Are

  • Warm & Welcoming
  • Expert & Knowledgeable
  • Empathetic & Caring
  • Confident & Assured
  • Personalised & Attentive
  • Empowering & Uplifting
  • Elegant & Refined

We Are Not

  • Clinical or Cold
  • Salesy or Pushy
  • Jargon-Heavy
  • Overly Casual
  • Generic or Vague
  • Exclusionary
  • Alarmist or Fear-Based
Brand Tagline
"Beauty That Never Ages"
Brand Mission
"A place that prioritises beauty and wellness through advanced skin solutions — customised skincare treatments targeting different skin types and conditions."
Trust Proof Point
"Over 20 years of professional experience, ensuring you feel right at home while achieving your best skin."

Messaging Principles

Lead with Outcomes

Focus on how clients will feel and look, not just what the treatment does. "Glowing, confident skin" over "laser procedure."

Personalisation First

Always emphasise that treatments are tailored and bespoke. "No two skins alike" is a core differentiator.

Trust & Expertise

Reference credentials (FDA & CE-approved, 20+ years experience) naturally, without being boastful.
